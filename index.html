About Me

I am a Master’s student in Human–Computer Interaction at the University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Dr. Fumeng Yang in the FIGX Lab.

My research explores how humans and intelligent systems can collaborate through transparent, explainable, and affective interfaces. I aim to make complex AI systems understandable and meaningful to people—empowering users to not only use AI, but also reason with it.

Broadly, my interests lie in human–AI decision-making, information visualization, and interpretability. I design and study interactive systems that bridge algorithmic reasoning and human understanding, with applications ranging from scientific knowledge exploration to accessible and inclusive design for diverse user groups.
